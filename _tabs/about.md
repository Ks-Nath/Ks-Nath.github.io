# ABOUT 

Hi. I’m someone who somehow ended up running two startups while also trying to become a doctor.
Yes, I’m aware that sounds like a bad life decision. We move.

I’m currently a medical student who thought, “You know what would make this harder? Entrepreneurship.”
So now I juggle exams, projects, deadlines, and the occasional existential crisis—usually all before lunch.
Why do I still do it then? Honestly, No fucking idea. I guess I'm weirdly hardwired to love this shit. 

This blog exists because my brain is… loud.

At any given moment, I’m thinking about business ideas, why I stopped running after a marathon, gym progress, or why I opened Instagram in the first place. Writing is my way of organizing that chaos into something that (hopefully) makes sense.

Also, it forces me to create instead of just consuming content like a well-trained algorithm victim.
